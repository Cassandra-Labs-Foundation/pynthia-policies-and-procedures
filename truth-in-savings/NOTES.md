# Truth in Savings — Drafting Notes

## Reference doc value (Patrick)
*"Solid background information to form the basis of a policy. Lack of detail."*

→ The BVS sample (`Truth in Savings Act Policy.docx.md`) is a **high-level outline** that touches every Reg DD requirement area but doesn't operationalize any of them. Use as section scaffolding; add the specifics.

## What the reference covers (well)
A complete enumeration of Reg DD requirement areas:
- General disclosure requirements
- Account disclosures (timing: at opening, or within 10 business days if remote)
- Subsequent disclosures (30-day advance notice for adverse changes)
- Maturity notices for rollover CDs and long-term accounts
- Periodic statement disclosures
- Interest payment calculation methods (daily balance / average daily balance)
- Advertising requirements ("free" rules, bonus disclosures)
- Record retention (2 years)
- Overdraft service disclosures (Reg DD § 1030.11)

## What the reference lacks (drafting work needed)
- **No specifics**: actual disclosure timing requirements, what fields must appear, APY calculation formula (the regulation requires APY = `100 [(1 + Interest/Principal)^(365/Days_in_term) - 1]`).
- **No procedures**: who reviews advertising, who approves disclosure changes, escalation when an error is found.
- **No interaction with overdraft opt-in** — Reg DD § 1030.11(c) overdraft service disclosure + Reg E § 1005.17 opt-in for ATM/one-time debit overdraft fees. These intersect; policy must reference both.
- **No examples** of acceptable APY / APYE wording, advertising boilerplate.

## DFPI scope
- **Federal Reg DD (12 CFR 1030)** applies regardless of regulator. Keep.
- **California Financial Code** has parallel state-level requirements for state-chartered institutions; check § 851 et seq. for state disclosure overlays.
- **CCFPL UDAAP** — DFPI examines truth-in-savings disclosures for deceptive practices.
- **National Credit Union Act** Part 707 only applies if Pynthia is a federal credit union — for DFPI-chartered state credit unions, Reg DD applies directly.

## Canonical drafting work needed (replace stub)
Suggested structure:
1. **Purpose & Scope** — applies to all deposit accounts offered to consumers.
2. **Disclosure Standards** — content, format ("clear and conspicuous"), language, delivery channels (paper / electronic / e-SIGN consent).
3. **Pre-Opening Disclosure** — required fields (APY, interest rate, compounding, minimum balance, fees, transaction limits).
4. **Subsequent Disclosure** — 30-day advance notice triggers.
5. **Maturity Notices** — rollover CD notices (10 days before maturity if term > 1 year; varies by structure).
6. **Periodic Statements** — required disclosures if statements provided.
7. **Interest Calculation** — formula, method (daily vs. average daily balance), when interest begins to accrue.
8. **Advertising** — pre-publication review, "free" account rules, APY/bonus disclosure triggers, online/social media coverage.
9. **Overdraft Disclosures** — Reg DD § 1030.11 statement aggregations + Reg E § 1005.17 opt-in interaction.
10. **Recordkeeping** — 2-year minimum (cross-reference `record-retention/`).
11. **Training & Compliance** — annual training for front-line staff, internal monitoring, periodic audit.
